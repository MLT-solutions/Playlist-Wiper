### Generic Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy for Playlist Wiper**

**Effective Date:** 30 Nov 2025

**1. Data Collection**
Playlist Wiper functions as a local, offline automation utility. The application **does not** collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal user data, keystrokes, or screen contents to external servers.

**2. Local Storage**
The application runs in temporary memory (RAM). No permanent logs of your automation history are stored on your hard drive after the application is closed.

**3. Third-Party Links**
The application contains a link to open a web browser for voluntary donations (PayPal). Clicking this button opens your default system browser; no payment data is processed within the app itself.

**4. Contact**
For privacy concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
